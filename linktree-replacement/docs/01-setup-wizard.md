# Setup Wizard

This is your main entry point for building your link page. Follow each step in order.

## Wizard Steps

### Getting Started
1. [Create Framer Account](02-framer-account.md) - Sign up for free
2. [Project Setup](03-project-setup.md) - Create your link page project

### Design Your Page
3. [Theme Selection](04-theme-selection.md) - Choose your colors and style
4. [Profile Setup](05-profile-setup.md) - Add your photo and bio
5. [Adding Links](06-adding-links.md) - Add your links and socials

### Optional Enhancements
6. [GA4 Setup](07-ga4-setup.md) - Track page views (optional)
7. [GTM Setup](08-gtm-setup.md) - Advanced tracking (optional)

### Go Live
8. [Publishing](09-publishing.md) - Launch your page
9. [Optional Components](10-optional-components.md) - Fun extras like EyeTracker
